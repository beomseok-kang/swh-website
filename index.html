So What Happened?
"So What Happened?" is a relay novel platform, available for Android.
The app is being serviced in South Korea.
Join a relay novel and show your creativity.
It is free to use and everybody is welcome here.
Download App
Highly Interactive and Awesome UI
"So What Happened?" has a highly interactive and beautiful UI. The native flutter app will give you an awesome user experience with natural animation transitions.

Join Any Relay You want. Write Your Stories.
You can join either 'short' or 'long' relays, which you can write up to 100 / 500 words for each. Write and develop the relay stories and show us your creativity.
